Excursion Lab weeks

For the class HCI Technologies with Labweeks from June 3rd 2019 until June 14th 2019. During these two weeks I worked with my group on a project under the topic Augemented and Virtual Reality. Augmented Reality (AR) is a second layer over our own world when you use those kinds of apps, examples are Pok√©mon Go or Harry Potter Wizards Unite. This are games that use the camera of your phone an in a way puts a filter of our world so you can see their world layed over our world. Virtual Reality is when you get imersed into a new world created by someone else you can do this with Google Cardboard, Oculus Rift or some cheap Vr glasses you can buy at the Action. It is an app were you have to put on somethun g like glasses to get imersed into a new world.

Product

Our product is an AR Nerf shooting range were you look through a phone to see what your target is. Our group had the topic AR and VR and we made the decision to make an AR game. We wanted to make a game were you had to look through the phone to see what is on the target were you had to shoot at. The product relates to the topic AR and VR because for it to work we made real life makers that we put in Unity, we used this to make the app with Vuforia, so the phone could detect the markers and you would see what your target was.

The product works as following: we put sensors on the back of our markers that were connected to an ardruino that worked together with Unity. With the arduino connected to Unity we could see when the target was hit and you if got a point. The app was made with Unity and Vuforia was the software we used to put it on a phone so the app could be used. Once the app was opened and you focus on the markers targets apeared that we got from the internet our were made by me with Cinema 4d. When you put the phone on Nerf gun it gets easier to shoot because you don't have to hold the phone. When you point the gun at the markers the targets will apear on your screen and now you can play the game.
